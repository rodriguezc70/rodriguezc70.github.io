Abstract
 This website explains the history of arcade machines. It goes into details of how it was simpler to enjoy games back in the past. It goes on to show the more iconic games that kept the arcade thriving. Unfortunately home consoles would rise over and give arcades almost know reason to go to. It then goes on to explain the last ditch efforts made to keep arcades around. The arcade started as one of the best ways to play video games and now is considered a niche method.

Abstract
 The site goes and explains the early years of arcades. Its main purpose is to determine what keeps people and players from no longer wanting to stop playing arcade machines. Similar to the movies and now being streaming services, there are some cases that shows nostalgia. Explanations seem that people enjoy the experience they can get from not only a different environment but also that of a different game. The nostalgia these consoles bring makes it hard for people to allow arcade machines to phase out.
