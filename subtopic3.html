 eSports History: How it all began
Site Author
 Florian Larch
Abstract
 With the creation of video games, competition was around the corner. People love to make sports or outdoor games competitive so video games are no different. This website explains how even when the first arcade game was released, there was esports held for it. Esports is similar to how the NFL, MLB, and NBA are. They are all sports that can earn money. Tournaments and beating high scores would set the goal for people to improve their performance in the games. This would eventually, with time passing, reach to a level of intensity, such as the superbowl. Esports are a modern way that a person can make revenue.
